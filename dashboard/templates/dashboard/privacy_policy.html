Privacy Policy

Online Meter Test (OMT)

Effective Date: 2024

Palli Bidyut ("we," "our," or "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the information you provide while using the Online Meter Test (OMT) software for Electric Energy Meter Testing.

1. Information We Collect

  • User Information: Name, email, phone number, designation, and office location for account creation and authentication.
  • Meter Data: Test results, meter numbers, and associated readings.
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, and operating system for security purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To Operate OMT: Ensure proper functionality, manage test data, and maintain system accuracy.
  • To Improve Services: Analyze system performance and user feedback to enhance software features.
  • For Compliance: Meet legal and organizational requirements, ensuring data integrity.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • Within Palli Bidyut: To authorized personnel for official purposes.

We implement robust security measures to protect your data, including encryption, firewalls, and secure access protocols.

5. User Responsibilities
